# Milestone v1.26: Live Position Management And Pilot Learning

**Status:** SHIPPED 2026-06-21
**Phases:** 61-65
**Total Plans:** 5
**Requirements:** 13/13 satisfied

## Overview

v1.26 turned the running small-capital live pilot into an auditable position
lifecycle and learning loop. The milestone focused on explaining active
positions before scanning for new entries, making close/reduce actions
filter-aware and confirmation-gated, reconciling closed live outcomes into
operator-readable performance evidence, and deploying a read-only server packet
that proves the current pilot state without mutating Binance, env, systemd, or
strategy settings.

Manual positions, especially `BTWUSDT`, remained isolated throughout the
milestone.

## Phases

### Phase 61: Close-Review Exit Plan Repair

**Goal:** Explain and repair why agent-managed `close_review` positions can be
review-required but still fail to produce an executable close/reduce plan.

**Requirements:** POS-01, POS-02, POS-04
**Plans:** 1/1 plan complete
**Status:** Complete

**Success criteria:**

1. `ops time-exit-plan` or a companion diagnostic reports exact failed
   preconditions for each `close_review` position.
2. Agent-managed positions can produce filter-aware close/reduce plan
   candidates when Binance quantity/notional constraints allow.
3. Manual positions such as `BTWUSDT` remain classified as `manual_hold` and
   never become close/reduce candidates.
4. Unprotected or deteriorating positions are surfaced with higher urgency than

**Outcome:**

## Milestone Summary

**Key Decisions:**

- Position lifecycle decisions must be persisted before every new-entry scan.
- Close/reduce execution must rerun current evidence and require a fresh
  matching token.
- Live outcome reconciliation can be optional at report time, but default
  ledger reads stay DB-only and non-mutating.
- Guard feedback is recommendation-only; it cannot write env files, apply guard
  updates, place orders, cancel orders, or raise risk.
- Pilot learning packets compose existing read-only reports instead of
  duplicating Binance order or risk logic.
- `BTWUSDT` remains manual exposure and must not consume bot entry capacity or
  become a close/reduce candidate.

**Issues Resolved:**

- Agent-managed positions can now explain why they are `close_review`,
  `close_ready`, `blocked`, or `manual_hold`.
- Live cycles now record active-position stewardship before scanning for new
  entries.
- Closed live outcomes can be reconciled and attributed without manual
  symbol-by-symbol review.
- Server operators have one canary packet for lifecycle, cap, exit, ledger, and
  trace evidence.

**Issues Deferred:**

- The packet is not profitability proof; further risk increases require
  repeated positive closed live outcomes and drawdown/profit-factor gates.
- Automatic position management remains disabled unless the operator explicitly
  enables it.
- Richer social/news adapters and multi-regime strategy selection remain
  future milestone work.

**Technical Debt Incurred:**

- GSD milestone archive tooling mis-counted historical phases during the first
  v1.26 close attempt, so this archive was corrected manually to scope only
  Phases 61-65.
