# Plan 10-01: Short-Window Backtest Harness

## Objective

Add repeatable small-capital backtests before raising live limits in volatile
crypto futures markets.

## Scope

- Fetch public Binance USD-M kline datasets without secrets.
- Run a baseline hot-momentum strategy against local kline datasets.
- Use completed candles only, with next-candle-open entries.
- Include taker fees, slippage, contract-notional caps, per-trade risk caps,
  daily loss caps, and concurrent-position caps.
- Run staged short-window sweeps across strict, balanced, and aggressive
  variants.
- Keep raw data and generated reports in gitignored runtime paths.

## Verification

- Unit tests cover kline loading/fetch pagination, signal generation,
  next-open entry behavior, stop-first collision handling, staged sweeps, CLI
  fetch/run/sweep commands, and REST `startTime`/`endTime` parameters.
- Public-kline smoke runs cover both major coins and current hot-coin symbols.
- Full unit suite and `git diff --check` pass.

## Promotion Rule

Backtests are advisory evidence only. Do not raise live limits until staged
results are positive across several non-overlapping short windows and forward
timer evidence confirms protective orders after a real submitted entry.
