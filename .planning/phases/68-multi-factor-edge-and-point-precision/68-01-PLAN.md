# Plan 68-01: Multi-Factor Edge And Point Precision

<objective>
Make the deterministic quant setup rich enough that every trade or no-trade
decision can be inspected before the AI overlay: grouped factor evidence,
missing-input coverage, market-structure entry/stop/target diagnostics,
sizing/min-notional explanations, and recommendation-only guard feedback with
sample, recency, and decay context.
</objective>

<must_haves>

- D-01: Deterministic setup scoring remains the first authority. AI may review
  or veto, but it cannot bypass setup, sizing, exchange-filter, or risk gates.
- D-02: Factor output must explicitly expose trend/momentum, volume impulse,
  taker flow, open-interest change/value, funding, volatility/range, liquidity,
  tradability, and narrative heat. Missing data must be visible.
- D-03: Factor output must include directional polarity, weighted score,
  reasons, raw value, and compact summaries for candidate evaluations,
  persistence, AI context, and live-cycle explainability.
- D-04: Phase 68 must not increase size, leverage, concurrency, or risk caps.
- D-05: Entry, stop, and target must derive from market structure inputs where
  available: reference price, support/resistance, VWAP, ATR/realized
  volatility, close-position-in-range, and exchange filters.
- D-06: `price_basis` or equivalent diagnostics must include anchors, raw and
  capped distances, risk/reward, stop risk, min executable notional, exchange
  quantization pressure, and missing inputs.
- D-07: Liquidation-distance diagnostics may be approximate and conservative;
  full high-leverage optimization belongs to Phase 69.
- D-08: Small notional choices must be explainable through stop distance, risk
  cap, min-notional pressure, profile fraction, and configured max-position cap.
- D-09: Every candidate evaluation should expose why it traded or passed:
  factor threshold failures, profile blocks, missing feature coverage, point
  geometry failures, min-notional pressure, guard blocks, and AI pass reasons.
- D-10: Enrich existing setup, AI compact context, `candidate_evaluations`,
  live-cycle explainability, and trade traces instead of creating a separate
  trace system.
- D-11: Keep all outputs secret-safe.
- D-12: Outcome feedback remains recommendation-only or risk-reducing.
- D-13: Guard feedback must account for minimum samples, recency, and decay.
- D-14: Outcome grouping continues to distinguish symbol, side, setup reason,
  factor reason/name, exit reason, and hold bucket.
- D-15: `BTWUSDT` and configured manual symbols remain operator-owned and
  outside bot candidate/action management.
- D-16: Runtime caps must be read from config/risk inputs; do not hard-code
  server live-cap values.

</must_haves>

<tasks>

1. Enrich factor evidence shape in deterministic setup.
   - Read first: `src/bfa/strategy/setup.py`,
     `tests/test_strategy_setup.py`, and `src/bfa/ai/schema.py`.
   - Add a compact factor model summary to setup payloads: grouped factor
     contributions, missing factor/input coverage, directional contribution
     totals, threshold diagnostics, top supportive/opposing factors, and guard
     factor names/reasons.
   - Preserve existing factor names and scores for compatibility.
   - Add or adjust no-trade reasons when factor edge, factor coverage, or guard
     thresholds fail.
   - Do not raise default edge thresholds unless the change is strictly
     risk-reducing and covered by tests.

2. Capture open-interest change and feature coverage.
   - Read first: `src/bfa/strategy/features.py`,
     `tests/test_strategy_features.py`, and market collector snapshot shapes.
   - Track consecutive open-interest and open-interest-history values where
     available and expose `open_interest_change_percent` plus quality notes
     when missing.
   - Include OI change in `SymbolFeatures.to_dict()` and in AI compact
     candidate context.
   - Update `_oi_factor()` to score current OI value and OI change separately
     enough for operator-readable reasons.

3. Add market-structure geometry diagnostics.
   - Read first: `src/bfa/strategy/setup.py`,
     `src/bfa/strategy/indicators.py`, and `src/bfa/ai/schema.py`.
   - Extend stop/target helpers to retain raw, uncapped, capped, anchor, and
     profile-adjusted distances.
   - Extend `price_basis` with risk/reward, stop-risk fraction/USDT,
     min executable notional, min-notional pressure, max-position cap,
     risk-sized notional, profile fraction, exchange filters, and missing
     geometry inputs.
   - Add approximate liquidation-distance diagnostics from
     `RiskLimits.max_leverage` and side/reference/entry. Keep this
     informational and conservative.
   - Preserve exchange-filter safety; do not submit or deploy anything.

4. Surface trace diagnostics through existing live outputs.
