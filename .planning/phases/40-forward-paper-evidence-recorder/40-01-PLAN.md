# Plan 40-01: Forward-Paper Evidence Recorder

## Goal

Create a read-only forward-paper recorder that can collect out-of-sample
evidence for calibrated quant setup variants without placing orders or creating
order intents.

## Scope

- Add dedicated event-store categories for paper signals and paper outcomes.
- Add `ops forward-paper-run`.
- Fetch public klines for selected symbols and interval.
- Generate paper signals from a quant setup backtest variant.
- Avoid duplicate open paper signals for the same symbol, interval, and
  variant.
- Settle open paper signals using later bars with stop, target, time-exit,
  fees, and slippage.
- Verify no `order_intents` are written.

## Out Of Scope

- No live timer restore.
- No risk profile apply.
- No signed Binance client or exchange mutation.
- No automatic claim of profitability from paper evidence.

## Verification

- Focused forward-paper, event-store, and CLI tests.
- Full local test suite.
- Secret scan and diff check before commit.
