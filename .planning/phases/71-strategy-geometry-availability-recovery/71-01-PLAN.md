# Plan 71-01: Strategy Geometry Availability Recovery

**Milestone:** v1.28 Post-GSD Strategy Consolidation
**Status:** Implementation complete; verification pending server backtest.

## Context

The post-GSD live iterations (see `.planning/POST-GSD-LIVE-ITERATIONS.md`) added a
regime-routed fused strategy with a trend leg and a micro-grid/scalping leg. A
strict mathematical review of the full pipeline (candidate generation -> regime
routing -> leg geometry -> fusion -> sizing governor -> risk caps -> execution
-> sentinel exit) identified several layered conservatisms whose product drove
the end-to-end cycle trade probability below ~0.3%, matching the Phase 70
"submitted=false" live evidence.

This phase recovers availability without raising any risk cap, by repairing the
geometry math and the overfit/feedback controls that were starving the system.

## Changes

### GEO-01: Trend-leg RR geometry repair (P0-A)

`src/bfa/strategy/setup.py::_target_distance_percent`

The pre-improvement volatility ceiling `min(target, max(vol*2.0, stop*1.25))`
could collapse the target below `stop * min_risk_reward` in compressed
volatility, so the `min_risk_reward` gate could never be satisfied and the trend
leg was systematically untradeable. The ceiling now floors at
`stop * max(min_risk_reward, 1.0)` so it only trims excess reward. The Monte
Carlo confirmed the legacy trend leg had a 100% RR-block rate.

### GEO-02: Micro wick EV overfit controls (P0-B)

`scripts/run_micro_grid_research.py`

- `wick_ev_confidence_z`: 0.25 -> 0.85 (one-sided ~80% lower confidence bound).
- `wick_ev_min_fills`: 5 -> 12 (meaningful standard error).
- Added `wick_ev_walk_forward_enabled` (default True): fit the EV model on the
  earlier 2/3 of wick events, validate on the most recent 1/3, and downgrade to
  the quantile model when the out-of-sample slice is not profitable. Walk-forward
  is skipped when the validation slice is smaller than `wick_min_samples`, so the
  tightened z alone governs overfit control on sparse symbols.

### GEO-03: Outcome-health deadlock release (P1-A)

`src/bfa/execution/sizing.py::_outcome_health_multiplier`

Paper-guard blocks whose `outcome_count < 30` are downgraded to a downsize with
a hard floor of 0.5 (symbol, side, and factor blocks). This breaks the positive
feedback loop where thin negative evidence fully blocked trading, starving the
system of the fresh samples needed to ever clear the block. High-sample blocks
still hard-block.

### GEO-04: Fuser score magnitude normalization (P1-B)

`src/bfa/agent.py::_live_candidate_rank`

Micro candidates carried an artificial 80-point base offset (`80 + score*10`)
that dwarfed trend candidate scores (~10-50) and made the fuser prefer micro
unconditionally. The ranker now strips the base offset for micro candidates and
normalizes by 10, so regime routing decides leg priority rather than a magnitude
bias.

### GEO-05: Micro maker-in cost model (P2-A)

`scripts/run_micro_grid_research.py::round_trip_cost_percent`

Added an opt-in `entry_maker_cost` flag that costs the entry leg as maker plus a
small taker-risk buffer (accurate for post-only limit entries), with taker on
the exit leg. Legacy behavior is preserved exactly when the flag is False.

### GEO-06: Trend entry-quality relaxation (P3-A)

`src/bfa/backtest/models.py::quant_setup_live_action_flow`

`min_entry_quality_score` 5 -> 4 and `min_limit_entry_quality_score` 4 -> 3, so
the trend leg is reachable after the RR repair while still rejecting weak chasing
signals. The affected regression test was strengthened to keep its "weak chase
is rejected" semantics under the relaxed threshold.

## Monte Carlo Evidence

`scripts/run_montecarlo_strategy_sim.py` (synthetic GBM + mean-reverting paths,
research-only, no exchange/live state). Multi-seed (8000 paths x 4 seeds):

| Metric | Legacy | Improved | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| Trend RR-block rate | 100% | ~45% | recovered |
| Trend expectancy/fill | 0 (frozen) | +0.86U | positive |
| Trend availability/cycle | 0.00% | ~2.15% | recovered |
| Micro expectancy/fill | +0.016U (overfit) | +0.004U (honest OOS) | lower but real |
| Micro availability/cycle | 0.45% | ~0.90% | ~2x |
| End-to-end cycle trade prob | 0.45% | ~3.07% | ~6.8x uplift |

Note: the micro improved expectancy is lower than legacy on purpose - the legacy
figure was an in-sample overfit artifact; the improved figure is the honest
out-of-sample estimate and remains positive.
