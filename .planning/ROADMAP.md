## Active Phase Details

### Phase 66: Live Cycle Explainability And Ledger Cadence

**Goal:** Make every recent live cycle inspectable enough that the operator can
see why the bot did or did not trade and whether outcomes have been reconciled.

**Requirements:** OPS-03, LEARN-04

**Plans:** 1/1 plan complete
**Status:** Planned

**Success criteria:**

1. A read-only command/report lists recent live cycles with evaluated symbols,
   skip reasons, factor scores, AI decisions, risk decisions, sizing caps, and
   submitted-order status.
2. The report includes a concise explanation for small notional choices when
   stop distance, risk cap, margin cap, filters, or portfolio caps constrain
   size.
3. Closed live outcome reconciliation and ledger reporting can run on a single
   scheduled/single-command path with mutation proof showing no order/env/systemd
   or risk/guard application.
4. Manual symbols remain visible in diagnostics without being treated as
   bot-managed evidence.

### Phase 67: Adaptive Hot-Symbol Breadth And Guarded Queue

**Goal:** Broaden live hot-symbol observation while keeping weak-evidence,
tradability, and manual-symbol guards in front of AI and execution.

**Requirements:** SCAN-01, SCAN-02, SCAN-03, SCAN-04

**Plans:** 0/1 plan complete
**Status:** Not started

**Success criteria:**

1. Live hot-symbol selection can evaluate at least 80 current Binance USD-M
   USDT symbols subject to configured volume/liquidity/tradability floors.
2. Candidate source-health output records ticker, kline, open-interest,
   funding, taker-flow, and available narrative/manual/social-export inputs.
3. Recent weak live/paper evidence can suppress repeated symbols, sides, or
   factor patterns unless the operator resets or overrides the guard.
4. Later candidates are evaluated after retryable AI/setup/risk skips while the
   default live cycle still submits at most one order.

### Phase 68: Multi-Factor Edge And Point Precision

**Goal:** Upgrade deterministic setup scoring and point geometry so AI reviews a
well-formed trade/no-trade proposal instead of a thin hotness summary.

**Requirements:** EDGE-01, EDGE-02, EDGE-03, EDGE-04

**Plans:** 0/1 plan complete
**Status:** Not started

**Success criteria:**

1. Setup scoring combines trend/momentum, volume impulse, taker flow,
   open-interest change, funding, volatility/range, liquidity, and tradability
   factors before any AI overlay.
2. Entry, stop, and target diagnostics include risk/reward, stop distance,
   liquidation distance, exchange filters, and min-notional pressure.
3. Trade/no-trade traces explain factor thresholds and point geometry for the
   final action.
4. Outcome-driven factor guards use minimum sample counts, recency, and decay,
   and remain recommendation-only.

### Phase 69: Adaptive Sizing And High-Leverage Governor

**Goal:** Let position size adapt to signal quality and account state while
blocking high-leverage setups whose liquidation, volatility, spread, or
portfolio pressure is unsafe for the pilot.

**Requirements:** SIZE-01, SIZE-02, SIZE-03, SIZE-04

**Plans:** 0/1 plan complete
**Status:** Not started

**Success criteria:**

1. Dynamic sizing can increase or reduce notional within absolute caps using
   signal quality, stop distance, liquidity, volatility, available balance, and
   recent outcome health.
2. High-leverage entries are blocked or downsized when liquidation distance,
   stop distance, spread/slippage, or volatility fails configured floors.
3. Portfolio checks include account-level available balance and manual-position
   margin pressure without counting manual symbols as bot positions or bot exit
   candidates.
4. Any risk/sizing increase has a preview artifact, rollback path, and evidence
   gate; automated feedback may decrease risk but cannot increase it without
   explicit operator approval.

### Phase 70: Server Canary And Manual Boundary Verification

**Goal:** Deploy v1.27 to the isolated server and prove the broader scanner,
multi-factor traces, adaptive sizing, timers, and manual-position boundary work
under current live pilot conditions.

**Requirements:** OPS-04, OPS-05, RISK-05

**Plans:** 0/1 plan complete
**Status:** Not started

**Success criteria:**

1. Server deployment is limited to `/opt/binance-futures-agent` and
   `/etc/binance-futures-agent`.
2. Live and paper timers are restored after any deployment pause, and one-shot
   services are inactive after verification.
3. The server canary packet includes timer state, open positions, open algo
   orders, manual symbols, cap utilization, latest outcomes, and recent
   trade/no-trade traces.
4. `BTWUSDT` and configured manual symbols are excluded from bot entry capacity,
   auto-management, close/reduce execution, and candidate selection.
5. Server artifacts are scanned for sensitive fields before milestone closeout.
